Group Constitution

Preamble

This constitution is established to maintain a harmonious and respectful environment within our WhatsApp group. All members are expected to adhere to these guidelines to ensure a positive experience for everyone.

Section 1: General Guidelines

  • Respect and Kindness: Treat fellow members with respect and kindness. No form of harassment, bullying, or offensive language will be tolerated.
  • No Adult Content: No sharing of any adult content or adult drinks. This includes foul language, photos, or videos of alcohol, etc.
  • Relevance: Keep conversations relevant to the group's purpose. Off-topic discussions may be redirected by admins if they are inappropriate.
  • No Spamming: Refrain from posting repetitive messages, excessive emojis, or unnecessary content that disrupts the group.

Section 2: Content Sharing

  • Article (i): Members are encouraged to share valuable content related to the group's theme.
  • Article (ii): No links to other social media platforms except:
    • (i) YouTube: youtube.com (or its other domains like youtu.be)
    • (ii) WhatsApp: whatsapp.com (or its other domains [includes Meta/Facebook])
  • Article (iii): Links to external websites or resources should be relevant and safe. Admins reserve the right to verify and approve such links.

Section 3: Membership

  • Membership Conduct: Members are expected to follow the guidelines outlined in this constitution.
  • Removal: Admins reserve the right to remove members who consistently violate the rules or disrupt the group dynamic.

Section 4: Leadership Process

  • Random Selection: The group leader/admin will be chosen randomly from the existing members every week (it may not if there are problems).
  • Leadership Responsibilities: The leader will be responsible for enforcing the rules, maintaining order, and communicating important updates to the group.
  • Succession: If the leader is removed or steps down, a new leader will be randomly selected from the remaining members.

Section 5: Rule Amendments and Review Process

  • Review and Approval: Any new rule or amendment to the existing constitution must be reviewed and approved by at least one other admin or designated reviewer before implementation.
  • Transparency: Members will be notified of any changes to the rules or constitution, along with a brief explanation for the change.

Section 6: Final Note

By participating in this WhatsApp group, members acknowledge and agree to abide by this constitution. Let's work together to create a positive and engaging community!

Rules

  1. No use of strong and offensive language, and use of unacceptable or bad language.
  2. Everyone has 7 days of admin privileges, after that leadership will be rotated.
  3. No mangsho.
  4. No spamming.
  5. Respect the constitution.

If any rules are broken, then the user will be suspended in leadership (for 1 month).
